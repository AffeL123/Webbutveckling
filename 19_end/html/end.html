how to enter the end

To enter the End in Minecraft you first need to find a stronghold by using Eyes of Ender which are crafted from ender pearls and blaze powder throw the eyes and follow where they go until they lead you to the stronghold then explore it until you find the End Portal room the portal will have empty frames so place an Eye of Ender in each one to activate it once all frames are filled the portal opens and you can jump in to enter the End and fight the Ender Dragon

how to kill the enderdragont

To kill the Ender Dragon in Minecraft start by destroying the End Crystals on top of the obsidian pillars because they heal the dragon you can shoot them with a bow or carefully climb up and break them then fight the Ender Dragon by using a bow while it is flying and a sword when it lands on the portal keep moving to avoid attacks and watch out for Endermen then once its health is gone it will be defeated and the exit portal will appear

how to get an elytra

To get an elytra in Minecraft you first need to defeat the Ender Dragon and enter the End city area using an End Gateway then explore End Cities until you find one with a flying ship called an End Ship inside it you will find an item frame holding the elytra defeat the Shulker guarding it and take the elytra from the frame then you can equip it in your chest slot and use fireworks to fly around the world
