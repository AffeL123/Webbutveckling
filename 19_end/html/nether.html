how to get enderpearls

To get ender pearls in the Nether in Minecraft you can trade with Piglin by giving them gold ingots since they will sometimes give you ender pearls in return make sure you wear at least one piece of gold armor so they don’t attack you another way is to find warped forests where Enderman spawn more often and defeat them to get ender pearls but be careful because they can be dangerous to fight

how to get blazerods

To get blaze rods in Minecraft you need to go to the Nether and find a Nether Fortress where Blaze spawn from spawners defeat blazes using a sword or bow and they will drop blaze rods which you can collect be careful because they shoot fireballs and can set you on fire so bring armor food and maybe fire resistance to survive the fight

why you need enderpearls and blaze rods

You need blaze rods in Minecraft mainly to progress toward the end of the game because they are used to craft blaze powder which is required to make Eyes of Ender these are what you use to find and activate the End Portal to reach the End dimension and fight the Ender Dragon blaze powder is also used for brewing potions so blaze rods are essential for both beating the game and making strong potions
