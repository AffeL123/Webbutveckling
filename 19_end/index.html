the creator

Minecraft was created by Markus Persson, also called Notch, a programmer from Sweden. He started developing the game in 2009 and released early versions online. Later he founded Mojang Studios with Carl Manneh and Jakob Porsér to continue developing the game. Minecraft quickly became very popular around the world. In 2014, Mojang and Minecraft were bought by Microsoft, and the game is still updated today by the Mojang team.

Why Notch created minecraft

Markus Persson created Minecraft in 2009 because he wanted to make a simple sandbox game where players could explore, mine resources, and build anything they imagined. He was inspired by games like Infiniminer and Dwarf Fortress. At first it was just a small personal project, but the game quickly became very popular, so he continued developing it with Mojang Studios.

What is minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox game where players explore a large world made of blocks. The main idea of the game is to mine resources, craft tools, and build structures. Players can create houses, farms, cities, or anything they imagine. The game also has a survival mode, where players must gather food and defend themselves from creatures like Creeper and Zombie. Because the world is open and players can build freely, Minecraft is mainly about creativity, exploration, and survival.
