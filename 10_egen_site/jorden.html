Earth is the only known planet that supports life. Covered by vast oceans, green forests, and endless skies, it provides everything we need to survive. From its vibrant ecosystems to its changing climates, Earth reminds us how connected all living things are and how important it is to protect our planet for future generations.

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known world with liquid water on its surface. It has a layered structure composed of a solid inner core, molten outer core, mantle, and crust.

Earths atmosphere is made up mainly of nitrogen and oxygen, protecting life by blocking harmful radiation and regulating temperature. Its climate system is driven by solar energy and ocean-atmosphere interactions.
