the earth

The Universe began about 13.8 billion years ago with the Big Bang, a rapid expansion that created space and time. Since then, it has continued to expand and cool, allowing galaxies and stars to form.

On the largest scale, the Universe forms a cosmic web of galaxies and clusters connected by filaments of dark matter. Most of its mass and energy are invisible, made up of dark matter and dark energy.
