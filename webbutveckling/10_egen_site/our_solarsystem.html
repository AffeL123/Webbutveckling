The Solar System consists of the Sun, eight planets, dwarf planets, moons, asteroids, and comets. The planets are divided into terrestrial (rocky) and gas giant types, each with distinct compositions and atmospheres.

Asteroids, comets, and meteoroids are small Solar System objects composed of rock, metal, and ice. Some planets, like Saturn, have ring systems formed from dust and ice particles orbiting their equators.
